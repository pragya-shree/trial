# 🌱 Perishable Food Optimizer

A smart web-based platform designed to reduce food waste by tracking perishable items, monitoring expiry dates, and connecting food donors with NGOs for efficient food redistribution.

## 📌 Problem Statement

Millions of tons of food are wasted every year due to poor inventory management and inefficient distribution systems. At the same time, many people struggle with food insecurity.

This project aims to bridge that gap by:

- Tracking perishable food items and their expiry dates.
- Prioritizing items based on urgency.
- Enabling food donations before items expire.
- Connecting donors with NGOs for food redistribution.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📊 Smart Inventory Dashboard
- Track food items and expiry dates.
- View active, warning, critical, and expired items.
- Color-coded expiry monitoring system.

### 🍱 Food Donation Portal
- Submit food donation requests.
- Upload food images.
- Specify quantity, type, and pickup location.
- Indicate preparation time or expiry window.

### 🤝 NGO Registration System
- NGOs can register on the platform.
- Store organization details and serving capacity.
- Create a network of verified food distribution partners.

### 🌍 NGO Partner Showcase
- Display registered NGO partners.
- Highlight meal-serving capacities and locations.

### 🌙 Dark Mode Support
- Persistent theme switching using Local Storage.
- Modern and responsive UI.

### 📱 Responsive Design
- Works across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Users add food items with expiry dates.
2. The system categorizes items into:
   - Active
   - Warning
   - Critical
   - Expired
3. Surplus food can be donated through the donation form.
4. NGOs register on the platform.
5. Food donations are routed to NGOs before expiry.
